ðŸŽ¯ How To Play

  • Menu: Click Play button or press SPACE/ENTER
  • Game: Click, tap, or press SPACE to flap
  • Game Over: SPACE to restart, ESC for menu

Guide the bird through pipe gaps by tapping to flap. Score increases by 1 for each pipe successfully passed. Difficulty increases every 5 points with faster pipes, smaller gaps, and more frequent spawning. Try to beat your high score!
