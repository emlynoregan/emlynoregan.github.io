🎯 How To Play

Desktop Controls:

  • Movement: Arrow keys or A/D keys
  • Fire: Spacebar
  • Pause: P or ESC

Mobile Controls:

  • Movement: ◀ ▶ buttons (left thumb)
  • Fire: 🔥 button (right thumb)
  • Pause: ☰ menu button
